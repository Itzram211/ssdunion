Our Mission & Values

The South Sudan Student Union (SSS U-JU) at Jimma University serves as the central hub for all South Sudanese students studying across various faculties. Our mission is to foster a strong sense of community, preserve our rich cultural heritage, and support the academic and social welfare of our members.

Fostering Unity

We unite students from different South Sudanese states and backgrounds, ensuring everyone has a supportive family away from home.

Academic Support

We provide students with academic guidelines, study groups, and mentorship to help our members achieve academic success in their fields.

Cultural Preservation

Through regular events, we celebrate and share the rich, diverse cultures and traditions of South Sudan.
